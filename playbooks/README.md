## ðŸŽ¯ What are Playbooks?

Playbooks are step-by-step operational guides for common procedures. They provide:
- Clear objectives
- Sequential steps
- Decision points
- Troubleshooting
- Success criteria

**When to use playbooks:**
- You're performing a common task
- You want to ensure consistency
- You need a checklist to follow
- You're training someone new
